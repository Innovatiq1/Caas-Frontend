Today, CAAS celebrates the first-ever Multi-Regional Trajectory Based Operations (TBO) Demonstration Flight, using a Boeing 787-10 ecoDemonstrator Explorer! Together with The Boeing Company, the three-year long collaboration amongst four major air hubs, Japan, Singapore, Thailand and the United States, culminated in this demonstration flight. This is a major step forward in our joint effort towards sustainable aviation, aimed at improving flight efficiencies and reducing carbon emissions, with potential to cut an aircraft’s fuel burn by up to 10%.
